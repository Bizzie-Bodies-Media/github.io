Discovering How to Build
The Digital World

Step by step, we are learning how the internet works, how to write our first lines of code, and how to turn our creative ideas into interactive digital projects.

Hands-On Activities

Putting concepts into practice through guided, step-by-step projects.

Structuring Information

Learning the foundational building blocks of the web. We use basic HTML to organize text, insert images, and create links to share information clearly.

Creative Design

Exploring how to make things look great. We use CSS to add colors, change fonts, and adjust layouts so our digital creations look exactly how we imagine them.

Understanding Logic

Discovering how computers think. We practice breaking down big ideas into smaller, step-by-step instructions to understand how interactive elements work.

Essential Skills for the Future

Beyond just learning how to type code, we are developing critical thinking habits that apply to all areas of learning. The focus is on building a strong, versatile foundation.

  • 1
    Problem Solving Learning to identify mistakes, figure out why things aren't working, and try new solutions until we succeed.
  • 2
    Logical Sequencing Understanding that the order of instructions matters, helping to improve organization and clear thinking.
  • 3
    Digital Literacy Moving from just consuming content on the internet to understanding how it is actually made and structured safely.
