What can GrowBot do?

Built as an upgrade to static robotic farming systems, GrowBot navigates autonomously to manage micro-climates across dynamic environments.

Independent Navigation

Travels autonomously between outdoor growing beds and greenhouses, navigating clearances as narrow as 111cm.

Environmental Telemetry

Constantly monitors temperature, ambient light levels, atmospheric humidity, and soil moisture.

Precision Automated Irrigation

Utilizes a dynamic water-level monitoring system and smart water pump to water targeted plants only when needed.

Obstacle Avoidance

Integrates ultrasonic sensors that safeguard pathways, students, and valuable crops from physical impact.

Continuous Logging

Stores historical sensor logs for classrooms to parse, enabling cross-disciplinary data analysis and plant-biology research.

Future-Proof AI Upgrades

Built to support an onboard camera mount with expandable computing power for computer vision and weed/pest detection.

3
