About GrowBot

Project Overview

GrowBot is a mobile gardening robot inspired by FarmBot but designed specifically for our school garden and greenhouse.

The robot will monitor environmental conditions, automatically water plants, avoid obstacles and collect valuable growing data using sensors.

Project Objectives

Engineering

Design and build a fully functioning mobile robot capable of autonomous gardening.

Education

Develop students' practical skills in robotics, electronics, programming and mechanical engineering.

Sustainability

Encourage efficient water use, smart farming and environmental awareness.

GrowBot Features

🚜 Mobile Platform

Moves safely around the garden and greenhouse.

💧 Automatic Watering

Waters plants only when soil moisture levels are low.

📡 Smart Sensors

Temperature, humidity, soil moisture, light and water level monitoring.

📷 Camera Mount

Supports plant monitoring and future AI image recognition.

💡 Lighting Mounts

Mounting points for LED work lights or grow lights.

🌱 Sustainable Design

Rechargeable battery, efficient watering and upgradeable components.
